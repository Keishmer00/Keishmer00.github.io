Project Overview

Nexus Project is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to facilitate seamless interactions between users and various services.

Role and Responsibilities

As the lead UX/UI designer, I was responsible for conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and developing a cohesive design system that ensures consistency across all platforms. My role also involved collaborating closely with developers to ensure the feasibility of designs and their successful implementation.

Design Process

The design process began with extensive user research to understand the needs and pain points of our target audience. This was followed by the creation of user personas and journey maps to guide the design decisions. Wireframes were developed to outline the structure of the applications, which were then transformed into high-fidelity prototypes using Figma. The final designs were tested with real users to gather feedback and make necessary adjustments.

Technologies Used

Figma for design and prototyping, Adobe Creative Suite for graphic elements, and collaboration tools like Slack and Jira for project management.
