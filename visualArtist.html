Correr la Tierra en Balsa

This project represents the immersion in the ancestral knowledge of our planet, guided by the nature of the Amazon jungle accompanied by the mastery of ayahuasca.

Here a path is traced from the ontological uncertainty in the identity of the subject that transforms in its transit the masks with which it defined itself, characteristics coming from the western society.

The raft represents the spiritual journey executed in the transformation of the individual in front of great knowledge of the universe
